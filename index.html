EcoRunner 3D
Version 3.0 · Your Eco-Action Adventure

An endless runner where every swipe is a step toward a greener planet — dodge pollution, collect eco items, and race for a sustainable future.

KEY FEATURES
🌲
Eco Item Collection

Collect trees, solar panels, recycle bins, water orbs, and wind turbines to boost your eco score and restore the environment.

🏃
Endless Runner Gameplay

Swipe left and right to dodge pollution obstacles like factories, smog clouds, and trash piles in an ever-accelerating city world.

🏆
Leaderboard & Progress

Compete globally, track your high scores, and climb the SDG-themed leaderboard to prove your eco-hero status.

📳
Customizable Experience

Toggle sound effects, music, and vibration to tailor your gameplay experience — anytime, anywhere.

SDG GOALS ALIGNMENT

EcoRunner 3D supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by raising environmental awareness, promoting responsible consumption, and inspiring players to take climate action through interactive play.

SDG 13
🌍
Climate Action

Every eco item collected symbolizes a real-world climate action — from planting trees to harnessing clean energy — encouraging awareness of climate change.

SDG 11
🏙️
Sustainable Cities

The game world depicts urban environments where players restore city health by removing pollution, promoting the vision of clean, livable cities for all.

SDG 12
♻️
Responsible Consumption

Players learn to distinguish eco-friendly choices from wasteful ones, reinforcing habits of mindful consumption and responsible resource use.

SDG 15
🌲
Life on Land

Collecting nature icons like trees and water orbs highlights the importance of protecting biodiversity and restoring healthy ecosystems on land.

SDG 3
❤️
Good Health & Well-being

Promotes an active, screen-positive lifestyle by making environmental stewardship fun and accessible — contributing to both mental engagement and health literacy for all ages.

Swipe through three lanes, collect eco items, and dodge pollution to restore the city to green!

🌲 Tree +10 pts
☀️ Solar Panel +15 pts
♻️ Recycle Bin +12 pts
💧 Water Orb +10 pts

🏭 Factory −❤️ Life

⭐ Scoring Tips
🔥
Build a Streak! Collecting eco items in a row multiplies your score bonus.
🌍
Eco Health Bar fills as you collect items — when full, the city transforms to green!
❤️
3 Lives per game. Lose them all and it's game over!
⬆️
Level Up after completing each stage — the city gets greener as you progress!
🏆
Unlock Achievements by reaching score milestones and collecting eco items!
